Core Features

OpenTMP LLM framework balances privacy, performance, and governance for distributed AI training.

Privacy-Preserving Training

Hybrid MPC protocol ensures data privacy during training and inference through secure aggregation techniques.

Fast MPC Engine

Optimized cryptographic computations reduce overhead while maintaining privacy guarantees for large models.

Advanced LLM Support

Comprehensive support for SFT, RLHF, DPO and other post-training algorithms with privacy protection.

Flexible Deployment

Support for local, master-slave, and peer-to-peer modes to adapt to various deployment scenarios.

Efficiency Optimization

PEFT methods, knowledge distillation, and quantization techniques for resource-constrained environments.

Multi-Party Governance

MPC-based joint training and shared model ownership for collaborative governance among multiple parties.
