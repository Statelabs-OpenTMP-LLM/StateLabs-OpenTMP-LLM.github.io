Key Highlights

OpenTMP LLM offers a secure, scalable, and privacy-preserving framework for distributed large language model (LLM) training.

Private Training

Ensures data privacy during training with encrypted communication and minimal metadata.

Regulatory Compliance

Built-in support for regulatory auditability with selective data disclosure for compliance checks.

High Performance

Optimized for large-scale training with support for parallelized computations and batch aggregation.

Scalable Infrastructure

Supports distributed, multi-party model training across cloud, on-premise, or hybrid environments.
