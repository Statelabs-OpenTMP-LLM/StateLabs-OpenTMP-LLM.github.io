OpenTMP LLM Framework

Secure, Efficient, and Governable Distributed Training for Large Language Models.

The OpenTMP LLM framework integrates federated learning with multi-party computation (MPC) cryptography, ensuring data privacy while enabling distributed training of large language models.

Key Features

Explore how OpenTMP addresses data privacy and training efficiency in distributed LLM environments.

Secure LLM Training

Combines federated learning with MPC to ensure data privacy during distributed training and inference.

Efficient LLM Performance

Advanced techniques such as Edge AI acceleration, model distillation, and quantization optimize model performance.

Governable LLM Training

Collaborative training with shared model ownership and MPC-based joint training enhances governance and security.
